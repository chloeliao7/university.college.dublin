### OVERVIEW OF TENSES
---
- Overview of Tenses
  - PRÉSENT                : conditions and facts in the present actions in the present ideas or actions that are generally valid future actions that the speaker is sure about
     ----- je/j’, aime, finis, dors, vends tu, aimes, finis, dors, vends il/elle/on, aime, finit, dort, vend nous, aimons, finissons, dormons, vendons vous, aimez, finissez, dormez, vendez ils/elles, aiment, finissent, dorment vendent
  
  - PASSÉ COMPOSÉ          : actions that took place in the past generally preferred in spoken language
     ----- je/j’	ai aimé	ai fini	ai dormi	ai vendu tu	as aimé	as fini	as dormi	as vendu il/elle/on	a aimé	a fini	a dormi	a vendu nous	avons aimé	avons fini	avons dormi	avons vendu vous	avez aimé	avez fini	avez dormi	avez vendu ils/elles	ont aimé	ont fini	ont dormi	ont vendu 
  
  - Irregular verbs        : 
     ----- je/j’ aimais finissais dormais vendais tu aimais finissais dormais vendais il/elle/on aimait finissait dormait vendait nous aimions finissions dormions vendions vous aimiez 
     finissiez dormiez vendiez ils/elles aimaient finissaient dormaient vendaient
  
  - PASSÉ SIMPLE           : actions that took place at a specified time used in narrative and literary texts
  - PLUS-QUE-PARFAIT       : action that took place before another action in the past (past anterior)
  - FUTUR_SIMPLE           : actions that should or will take place in the future also a polite form
  - L’IMPARFAIT            : actions that took place in the past generally preferred in spoken language 
  - FUTUR_COMPOSÉ          : actions that are about to take place actions for which an intention already exists
  - passé_antérieur        : past action that had occured directly before another action (passé simple) occurred in the past we mostly find it in literary texts
  - IMPARFAIT/PASSÉ COMPOSÉ : something that has just been done or has just happened if we refer to the present moment, venir is conjugated in the present
  - FUTUR_ANTÉRIEUR        : supposition about an action in the past supposition that an action will have been completed by a specified point in the future
  - PASSÉ RÉCENT           : something that has just been done or has just happened if we refer to a moment in the past, venir is conjugated in the imparfait
